The Eleronian Empire

The southernmost region, Eleron, is mostly infertile plains, but the further south it goes there are thick jungles that contain many mysteries. The northern regions of Eleron are ruled by King Augustus Sun, and his loyal advisors. The south is too wild to tamed by the Eleronian Empire. The Sun Dynasty has ruled Eleron for three centuries, and have outlasted all coup attempts throughout the centuries. They are a strict culture that restricts magic heavily. Only approved magic may be learned through schooling. They outright reject the Old Ways and its magic and punish those who dare mutter any history or lore about such subjects. Most of the proletariat are pacified and well-taken care of by the merchant class, who are skilled at what they do. The ruling classes have cutthroat relationships, and it gets dirty sometimes. The major cities are the capital Storm's End, Hearth of the Bay, Cape Ford, and Sicilia.

Durglagh

The swampy regions of Durglagh lie just beyond the Sea of Passwell, named after the city that connects Durglagh to Eleron. Durglagh has a bloody history. Switching between being a vassal of Eleron and The Torwoods, the people have had enough and just recently found a shaky peace and an unstable independence.
