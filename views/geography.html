The Disconnected World

The Gods that the world of Yulon have used it to settle disputes, quest for power, and foil less than favorable plots. Consequentially, the beings of this world are the victims of numerous calamities, and because of these calamities, the civilizations of each continent are separated entirely from one another by destructive storms, monstrous whirlpools, and sea demons. Forene is the most active of the four landmasses, and it holds the most advanced societies on this world.
